Welcome to My World

I'm Ayaat, and while I love makeup, singing, and dancing, my heart truly belongs to the incredible stories found in Anime and K-Dramas. I love how these shows can take us from heart-pounding action to deeply emotional moments that stay with us forever.

The Anime Universe

Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin)

This isn't just a show; it's a masterpiece of storytelling. I am fascinated by the complex themes of freedom, sacrifice, and the cycle of war. The character development of Eren Yeager and the intense animation make it an unforgettable experience that keeps me thinking long after the credits roll.

K-Drama Favorites

Twinkling Watermelon

This drama touched my heart with its beautiful story about family, music, and youth. The way it blends time travel with emotional healing is simply magical. It reminds me of the power of following your dreams.

Twenty-Five Twenty-One

A nostalgic journey about growing up and the bittersweet nature of first loves. The energy of Na Hee-do and the struggles of youth in the late 90s feel so real and inspiring. It’s a story about resilience and friendship.
