Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Ayaat. I am a person who finds beauty in the details—whether it's the perfect stroke of an eyeliner wing, the harmony of a melody, or the complex plot of a gripping story. I believe that life is a canvas, and I use my hobbies to paint it with as much color and emotion as possible.

My Creative World

Makeup & Drawing

For me, makeup and drawing are two sides of the same coin. I love the artistry of transformation—using a face or a blank sheet of paper to express a mood.

Singing & Dancing

Music is my escape. Singing allows me to express my inner voice, while dancing gives me the freedom to move and feel the rhythm.

Anime & K-Drama

I am a huge fan of storytelling. I dive deep into the worlds of Anime and K-Dramas, finding inspiration in their unique narratives.
